PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated:

#1. Introduction

YeMo API ("we", "our", or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our REST API service.

#2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP addresses for rate limiting and security purposes
  • Request methods, paths, and response times
  • User-Agent strings from HTTP headers
  • Timestamp of API requests
  • Error logs and debugging information

2.2 Information You Provide

When using certain endpoints (such as file upload), you may provide files or data. This information is processed according to the specific endpoint's functionality and is not stored longer than necessary.

#3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To enforce rate limiting and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the security and integrity of the Service

#4. Data Storage and Retention

We retain log data and request information for a reasonable period necessary to provide the Service, troubleshoot issues, and ensure security. Uploaded files are automatically deleted after 5 minutes. We do not store personal information beyond what is necessary for the operation of the Service.

#5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To prevent or investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service
  • With your explicit consent

#6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including rate limiting, IP-based access controls, and secure data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

#7. Cookies and Tracking

The Service does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. We rely on IP-based identification for rate limiting and security purposes only.
